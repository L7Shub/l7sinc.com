Money-Back Guarantee & Refund Policy

Effective: 2026-04-25  |  Entity: L7S Inc. (d/b/a L7 Solutions), a Georgia C-Corporation  |  Programs Covered: Credit Hero (H.O.T.7), CEO Hero, Loan Hero, Crypto Hero, Cashflow Hero, Coverage Hero, Community Hero — all tiers H.I.T. / R.E.A.L. / E.A.T.S. / O.M.N.I.

1. Our Guarantee

L7S Inc. stands behind every program with a results-aligned guarantee. If, after 180 days from program start, you have shown documented full cooperation with the program protocol and the engagement has produced no measurable improvement to the qualifying metric for your program (e.g., 3-bureau credit profile movement for Credit Hero / H.O.T.7), L7S Inc. will refund the program fee paid, less any third-party costs already disbursed on your behalf.

2. What "Cooperation" Means

  • Timely submission of all requested intake documents within 7 calendar days of request.
  • Authorization for L7S Inc. and its strategic partners to communicate on your behalf with the relevant bureaus, vendors, or service providers.
  • Maintaining accurate and current contact information.
  • Not engaging directly with disputed accounts or vendors in ways that interfere with program-managed actions during the engagement window.

3. Third-Party Costs Excluded From Refund

L7S Inc. is a productivity-tools, education, and outsourced-coordination provider — not a credit repair organization. Where program execution requires third-party services performed by accredited and compliant strategic partners (e.g., bureau access fees, dispute filing fees, document preparation fees, attorney consultations), those amounts are paid out to those partners on your behalf and are not refundable to L7S Inc. The refund issued by L7S Inc. covers the L7S program fee net of those disbursed third-party costs.

4. Outside the Guarantee Window

Refund requests received after the 180-day window, or for engagements where the cooperation criteria above were not met, are evaluated case-by-case. L7S Inc. may issue a partial credit toward another L7S program, a service-extension, or a written explanation of the denial — at L7S Inc.'s reasonable discretion.

5. Subscription Products

Recurring monthly subscriptions (L7S HERO Monthly, B.E.S.T. Basic / Professional / Enterprise) may be cancelled at any time and will not renew the following billing cycle. Already-billed monthly amounts for completed billing cycles are not pro-rated or refunded.

6. How to Request a Refund

Email hero@l7sinc.com with subject line "Refund Request — [Program Name] — [Your Name]". Include your enrollment date, program tier, and a one-paragraph summary of your cooperation history. We will acknowledge within 3 business days and resolve within 14 business days.

7. Compliance

This policy is consistent with the Georgia Fair Business Practices Act and the federal Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA), recognizing that L7S Inc. provides productivity tools, education, and coordinated access to compliant strategic partners — not direct credit-repair services. Direct credit-repair, consumer-law, tax, accounting, and legal services are performed by L7S Inc.'s accredited strategic partners under their own engagement agreements and refund terms.
